# Privacy Policy

**Round Timer**

Last updated: January 8, 2026

## Overview

Round Timer is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.**

- No personal information
- No usage analytics
- No tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party SDKs

## Data Storage

All app data (presets, workout history) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Round Timer uses no third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

- **Notifications** - To alert you of timer phase changes when the app is in the background
- **Siri & Shortcuts** - To allow hands-free timer control via voice commands

These permissions are optional and the app functions fully without them.
