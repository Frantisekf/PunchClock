# Privacy Policy for Punch Clock

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Overview

Punch Clock is a boxing and MMA round timer app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

Punch Clock does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, photos, or files
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Connect to the internet
- Share any data with third parties

## Data Storage

The only data stored by Punch Clock is your custom workout presets (timer configurations). This data is:
- Stored locally on your device only
- Never transmitted anywhere
- Deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

Punch Clock does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

Punch Clock does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
